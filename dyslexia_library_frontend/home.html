About us

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a learning difference that affects how people read and process text. It has nothing to do with intelligence — yet it goes undiagnosed and unsupported in millions of students worldwide.

The Silence Around It

Most people with dyslexia never openly talk about it. The fear of being judged, labelled as slow, or dismissed by teachers pushes them to struggle alone — silently falling behind in academics.

What Readapt Does

Readapt adapts educational content to the reader — not the other way around. With AI-powered rephrasing, customizable reading environments, and assistive tools, reading finally feels possible.
